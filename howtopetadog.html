    • Petting a dog

    This task requires nothing specifc except for:

    • your hand
    • a dog (again, doesn't have to be yours)

    As you can see from the chart below, dogs appreciate petting anywhere.

    A few specifc places your dog will especially appreciate are:

    • their hear (especially behind their ears)
    • their belly
    • their chest

    First place your hand on the dog's head, and then stroke downwards to their back. Continue this motion for as long as you (and the dog) feel is neccesary.
