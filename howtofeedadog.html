    • Feeding a dog

    To begin this task, obtain the following items

    • dog food
    • measuring cup
    • a bowl or dish
    • a dog (doesn't have to be yours)

    Before you start, make sure to examine this chart to see how much you should be feeding your dog.

    Once you know how much to feed your dog, take the measuring cup and put as much food as needed into the dog's bowl. Place the bowl in front of the dog and allow him time to eat.
